## ‚ö° 3-Step Setup (5 minutes)

### Step 1: Configure Google Login

1. Go to: https://console.cloud.google.com/apis/credentials
2. Click on your **OAuth 2.0 Client ID**
3. Under **"Authorized redirect URIs"**, click **"+ ADD URI"**
4. Add this URL exactly as shown:
   ```
   https://taxplanner.app/login.php
   ```
5. Click **SAVE**
6. Wait 10 minutes for Google to update

### Step 2: Test Login

1. Open: https://taxplanner.app
2. Click **"Sign in with Google"**
3. Choose your Google account
4. Click **"Allow"**

### Step 3: Start Using

## ‚ùì Common Issues

### "Redirect URI Mismatch" Error?
- Check the redirect URI is exactly: `https://taxplanner.app/login.php`
- No spaces before or after
- Wait 10 minutes after saving

### Can't Log In?
- Add your email to "Test users" in Google Console
- Go to: https://console.cloud.google.com/apis/credentials/consent

### Page Won't Load?
- Clear browser cache (Ctrl+Shift+Delete)
- Try incognito/private mode
- Use Chrome browser (recommended)
